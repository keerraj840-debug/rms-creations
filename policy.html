Privacy Policy

We respect your privacy. Any personal information shared with us through WhatsApp, email, or our website is used only to respond to your enquiry and prepare your order.

  • We do not sell or share your personal details with third parties.
  • Information is used only for order discussion, confirmation, and communication.
  • You may contact us anytime to request updates or removal of your details.

Terms & Conditions

All orders are accepted only after a clear discussion on WhatsApp. Final pricing and product details must be confirmed before the hamper is prepared.

  • All products are subject to availability and may vary slightly in appearance.
  • Custom requests are accepted based on feasibility and availability.
  • RMS Gift Hampers reserves the right to decline or modify requests if required.

Refund Policy

Refunds are only considered in exceptional cases where an order could not be fulfilled due to our side or where the wrong product was delivered.

  • Refund requests must be raised promptly after the issue is noticed.
  • Refunds are evaluated case by case and may require proof of the issue.
  • Once a custom hamper has been prepared and confirmed, refunds may not be applicable.

Cancellation Policy

If an order is cancelled before preparation begins, a partial or full refund may be offered depending on the stage of preparation and payment status.

  • Cancellation requests should be shared on WhatsApp as soon as possible.
  • Customised hampers prepared or packed may not be eligible for cancellation.
  • Any cancellation decision will be communicated clearly to the customer.
